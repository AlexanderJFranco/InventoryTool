Refer to the following for information and application:

This system is comprised of 4 main tabs and functionalities

Service Overview :

A complete overview of all customers and which months they have been serviced

Monthly Servicing :

On this tab one can alter the majority of the elements shown by simply

clicking on the element, typing in new values, and pressing enter.

* It is important to note that you can not alter more than 1 customer at one time *

Add Customer :

This tab allows for you to enter a new name for a customer and a new entry for them will be added

Remove Customer :

This tab allows for you to enter a name for a customer and the entry for them will be deleted
